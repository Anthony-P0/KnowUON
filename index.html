Welcome to KnowUon

Welcome new students

KnowUON is a website built by technology students who are in their final year, built for first year students to help you prepare and be ready for your time as a student at the University of Newcastle.

This website has many features that you can use to get started at university, from student resources like where to find coversheets for your assignments to knowing your way around the university by using our interactive map. These are the following features you can use on our website, start by using the left side-bar menu:

  • What to Know: This is where all the information regarding orientation, what you need to know before week 1 etc., is kept.
  • Directory: The directory has information regarding where to find specific staff members, courses, campuses etc.
  • Student Resources: Student Resources provides links to course coversheets as well as information on library guides.
  • UON Interactive Map: An interactive map of the Callaghan campus.
  • FAQs: These are the most frequently asked questions asked by students with answers provided.
  • Student Testimonials: Where past students have described their experiences at the university.
  • Support Services: Students can find various personal and academic support.
  • Contact Information: Provides contact information for all campuses.

If you are unsure and/or want further assistance please also follow us on our various social media channels. Instagram, Youtube, Twitter, LinkedIn and Facebook.
