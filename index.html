Welcome to KnowUon

Welcome new students

KnowUON has been designed with the intention of assisting new students during the orientation process at the University Of Newcastle. On the left hand side of the website are several pages for students to navigate with all the necessary information.

  • What to Know.This is where all the information regarding orientation, what you need to know before week 1 etc is kept.
  • Directory.The directory has information regarding where to find specific staff members, courses, campuses etc.
  • Student Resources. Student Resources provides links to course coversheets as well as information on library guides.
  • UON Interactive Map. An interactive map of the Callaghan campus.
  • FAQs.These are the most frequently asked questions asked by students with answers provided.
  • More. Student Testimonials where past students have described their experiences at the university.
  • Support Services. Students can find various personal and academic support.
  • Contact Information. Provides contact information for all campuses.

If you are unsure and/or want further assiatnace please also follow us on our various social media channels. Instagram, Youtube, Twitter, LinkedIn and Facebook.
